INTRODUCTION:

AHOF (All-Time Hall of Famer) is a nine-member South Korean boy group under F&F Entertainment. The group was formed through the survival show Universe League. The final lineup of AHOF consists of seven members from Team Rhythm, which won the "Prism Cup" in the competition. These members are JL, Steven, Shuaibo, Juwon, Jeongwoo, Chih En, and Daisuke, with two additional members, Woongki from Team Beat and Han from Team Groove.
